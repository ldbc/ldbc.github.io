Eighteenth TUC Meeting

  1. Home
  2. Eighteenth TUC Meeting
by Shipeng Qi / on 24 Apr 2024
Location: Guangzhou
Event dates: 30 Aug 2024 09:00 -- 31 Aug 2024 18:00 (local timezone)

Organizers: Shipeng Qi, Wenyuan Yu (Alibaba Damo), Yan Zhou (CreateLink)

LDBC is hosting a two-day hybrid workshop, co-located in Guangzhou with VLDB 2024 on August 30-31 (Friday-Saturday).

The program consists of 10- and 15-minute talks followed by a Q&A session. The talks will be recorded and made available
online. If you would like to participate please register using our form.

LDBC will host a social event on Friday at the 3F Xingang Room, the Westin Pazhou,
Guangzhou. The address is Area C, Canton Fair Complex, 681 Fengpu Zhong Rd., Haizhu District, Guangzhou, Guangdong

In addition, Ant Group, Alibaba Damo, and CreateLink will host a Happy Hour on Saturday. Join us by the Pearl River at the Vino Restaurant, located on the 8th Floor, No. 118 Modiesha Street, Area B, Beer Culture Creative Art District, Guangzhou.
