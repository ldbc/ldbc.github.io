LDBC SNB Business Intelligence workload

  1. Home
  2. LDBC SNB Business Intelligence workload

The LDBC SNB Business Intelligence workload is focusing on aggregation- and join-heavy complex queries touching a large portion of the graph with microbatches of insert/delete operations. Its data sets are available in Cloudflare R2 and in the SURF/CWI repository.

For an overview of the workload, see the VLDB 2023 paper and its presentation by Gábor Szárnyas. For a detailed specification, see the LDBC Social Network Benchmark specification.

The workload produces scoring metrics for performance (power and throughput scores) at the given scale and price/performance metrics. The full disclosure reports (FDRs) further break down the composition of the metric into its constituent parts, e.g. single query execution times.

The table below contains audited results. Note that the system cost indicates the 3-year TCO (total cost of ownership), which is the sum of the license, hardware, and maintenance costs (where maintenance means 24/7 support with a response time of less than 4 hours).
